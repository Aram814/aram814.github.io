QueerSafeSpace App

The QueerSafeSpace App was developed to aid in the safety of the LGBTQ+ community. It allows users to anonymously update locations, marking them with safety ratings, tags, and additional details to foster a safer environment.

My Role: UX Designer, Concept Developer
Methodologies: Inclusive Design Principles, User Research (Community-focused), Anonymous Reporting Systems, Location-based Services UX, Prototyping.
