Send Me On My Way

Role: UX Designer, Visual Designer
Tools: Figma, HTML/CSS, FlutterFlow
Live Site: SendMeOnMyWay.info

Overview

Send Me On My Way is the digital presence for a Florida-based death doula. My goal was to create a soft, comforting, and approachable site that reflected the sacred nature of end-of-life care without being clinical or cold.

Problem

Many people are unfamiliar with what a death doula is, and those searching for one often feel overwhelmed or anxious. The existing digital options for death doulas are often impersonal, confusing, or dated.

Approach

I worked closely with the practitioner to define a tone that felt natural: gentle, spiritual, and rooted in nature. I used an earthy color palette and flowing design language to reduce anxiety and welcome exploration. The site is fully mobile-responsive and integrates Calendly for appointment booking.

Reflection

This project taught me the importance of emotional tone in UX — how even layout, spacing, and font choices can build trust. Working on this site was a reminder that good UX isn’t just about usability; it’s about compassion.
