QueerSafeSpace

Community-Sourced Safety App for LGBTQ+ People

Overview

QueerSafeSpace is a community-powered map that allows LGBTQ+ users to identify, rate, and share safe or unsafe locations based on lived experience. The app's goal is to empower marginalized communities through collective insight and visibility.

Project Context

This case study was completed as part of my UX Design Certificate coursework. The app concept is original and reflects both my technical skills and my values as a queer designer seeking to serve underrepresented communities.

My Role

I led end-to-end UX design including competitive analysis, user research, persona creation, journey mapping, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. I also created a live prototype in Figma.
