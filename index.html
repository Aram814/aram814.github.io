About Me

Hi, I’m Amanda (she/her) — a UX designer who brings creativity, curiosity, and care to everything I build.

I design digital experiences that to tackle complex problems into user-centric solutions for real people — especially those often left out of traditional systems. I enjoy focusing on inclusive, intuitive solutions grounded in research and empathy that can genuinely improve lives.

I specialize in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. My style is bold but thoughtful - little retro - always human. I love working on projects where the details matter — from microinteractions to the way a user feels when they hit “submit."

When I'm not designing, I enjoy traveling, being in the outdoors, vibing to either emo-punk or classical strings, or simply sinking into a good book.

Let’s connect and build something that makes a difference to people!
